### Multimedia
1. **Capture Photo**: Open the camera app to capture a photo.
   ```bash
   am start -a android.media.action.IMAGE_CAPTURE
   ```
2. **Capture Video**: Open the camera app to record a video.
   ```bash
   am start -a android.media.action.VIDEO_CAPTURE
   ```
